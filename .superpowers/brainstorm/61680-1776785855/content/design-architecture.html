Page Sections (top → bottom)

  1. Hero — full-screen photo, date, venue, floating music button placeholder
  2. Love Story — "OUR LOVE STORY" heading + photo + Chinese romantic text
  3. Invitation Letter — Chinese poem text + "2026.11.14 我们，婚礼见~"
  4. Calendar — November 2026 grid, 14th highlighted with heart
  5. Countdown — "SAVE THE DATE" + live days/hours/min/sec timer + two historical photos (2017, 2019)
  6. Photo Gallery — horizontal scrollable strip of couple photos + "FOREVER AND EVER" text
  7. RSVP — Name / Pax / Phone form → submits to Google Sheets
  8. Footer — "SEE YOU SOON / 我们期待与您共度美好时刻"
